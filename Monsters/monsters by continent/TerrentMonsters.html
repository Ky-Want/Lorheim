Volare

The Volare have a unique poison that they secrete from their talons that slowly and painfully petrifies those it attacks. Unfortunately, they are not immune to this poison which just so happens to flow through their bodies, so as they grow older and their immune systems get weaker, the poison takes hold and begins turning them to stone until eventually they can't fly any longer and either crash to the ground, shattering into millions of bloody pieces, or simply can't take off anymore and finish the agonizing process on the ground.

Corbinus

On Terrent there are many species you may want to avoid. Say hello to the number 1 swampland dwelling member of that category, the Corbinus. These creatures stand between 6 and 7 feet tall and are highly aggressive. Thankfully they avoid socializing except to procreate, but it's still not going to be a fun time if you run into one. Not only are they very fast, they also have an extremely keen sense of smell and have incredibly good memory.

Elnius

Say hello to Terrent's deer equivalent. Following the demons that spilled through the rift, some animals made it through as well. One of the ones that survived the change in environment are the Elnius that made it across the water and now freely roam the forests of Terrent. While not as dangerous as some of the many demonic beings and animals that came into this realm, they're still predatory and highly territorial. They're actually the reason behind the vast majority of the deer populace disappearing from Terrent's forests. So they might not go out of their way to attack creatures they perceive as stronger than them, they will most definitely go after any threats to themselves and their territory and hunt down weaker prey to snack on.
