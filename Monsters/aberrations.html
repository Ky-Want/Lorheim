Crystalline Beasts

When Arcane mana is especially dense in an area it has a chance to cause abnormalities in the surrounding plant and animal life. The crystalline beasts are just a product of this, occurring when crystals that have absorbed massive amounts of mana imprint on a passing creature, often a beast such as a wolf or bat or some other similar animal that frequents caves that might house such crystals.

Once the cluster of crystals have imprinted, the transformation can take anywhere from mere weeks to entire years to complete, but at the end of it the crystals will have transformed into a fully matured, crystalline version of whatever creature it was that they imprinted on.

As they grow and begin moving around they slowly absorb information about the world around them, starting out at the intelligence level of an infant, and eventually becoming just as smart as a human.

Living Corpses

Living corpses are beings who started out alive, but had their souls slowly and agonizingly replaced by a perfect artificial replica made with Naturae magic. Living corpses are created by and prized amongst high level necromancers who lack morals. They look like and act like normal, living beings, and thus can blend in almost seamlessly with society. Some who are more sensitive to Naturae mana might get a general feeling of wrongness when interacting with a living corpse, but aside from that it's incredibly difficult to tell them apart from other people.
