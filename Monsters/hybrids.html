Shadikah Demons

The Shadikah Demons are the hybrid beasts born from the Clockwork war beasts of Kaemen and the Demons that came through the Rift. 56 AR is when the demons began breeding and creating more of their kind, not just with their own kind and the clockwork beasts, but other races as well. After their numbers started increasing even more, the Demon Hunters and Adventurer guilds gained more popularity.

The Shadikah are an exceptionally dangerous breed considering their lack of need for food and rest. Fortunately they are mostly concentrated in the desert regions of Kaemen, so not many encounter them in their lifetimes unless they explicitly go out of their way to hunt them down. That being said, they have been mostly free to run rampant and create an increasing number of offspring because of the lack of involvement from the hunters and adventurers.
