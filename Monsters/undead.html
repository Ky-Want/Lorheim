Living Corpses

Living corpses are beings who started out alive, but had their souls slowly and agonizingly replaced by a perfect artificial replica made with Naturae magic. Living corpses are created by and prized amongst high level necromancers who lack morals. They look like and act like normal, living beings, and thus can blend in almost seamlessly with society. Some who are more sensitive to Naturae mana might get a general feeling of wrongness when interacting with a living corpse, but aside from that it's incredibly difficult to tell them apart from other people.
