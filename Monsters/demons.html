Satrix

These 3 foot tall spider demons have become quite common in the forests of Terrent. The prefer to dwell in the trees, using their two longer, more flexible legs to move about swiftly along the branches as they search for their next meal. They often travel in groups of 3 to 4 and use their two fins (which can fan out to about a foot in width) to scent the air and help guide them down when they ambush prey. They're not the brightest though, so the largest threat they pose are their frequent breeding habits and constant attacks on literally anything that moves.

Black Dogs

Black Dogs are just one of many of the demonic creatures that made their way through the rift and across the ocean to Terrent. They are scavengers mostly, but won't hesitate to take out easy prey like children and small farm animals.

These creatures stand at 4ft tall at the shoulder and possess flexibility like a ferret paired with stiff, sharp threads of fur. They have a keen sense of smell and wander in packs for added protection from other predators. Also when hunting they laugh, much like hyenas, to communicate the location of prey to the rest of their pack.
