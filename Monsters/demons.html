Satrix

These 3 foot tall spider demons have become quite common in the forests of Terrent. The prefer to dwell in the trees, using their two longer, more flexible legs to move about swiftly along the branches as they search for their next meal. They often travel in groups of 3 to 4 and use their two fins (which can fan out to about a foot in width) to scent the air and help guide them down when they ambush prey. They're not the brightest though, so the largest threat they pose are their frequent breeding habits and constant attacks on literally anything that moves.
